▶ Manual 
When a person comes in front of a kiosk the system speaks welcome messages requests the voice command from that person. When the system successes in recognizing the voice command, it provides the information on what he wants.

If it fails to recognize that command, the system displays the menu in tile-arrayed format.

Instead of clicking a menu button directly, we capture a finger motion in air and decide which button he selects.
After choosing the touch button on what he wants, the kiosk operates in a normal way and pops up the information that he asks for.
